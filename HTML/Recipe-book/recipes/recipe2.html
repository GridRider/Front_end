Recipe 2 : Chocolate Shake

Ingredients:

  1. milk
  2. honey
  3. Chocolate icecream
  4. chocolate syrup
  5. banana
  6. cherry
  7. cashew nut
  8. chocolate chip

Steps:


            
  • Step 1:Add milk ,banana and chocolate chip to the blender and give it a nice blend
    • 
            
  • step2:Add honey to give sweetness.
    • 
            
  • step 3:In a serving glass add around 6 whole cashewnut
    • 
            
  • step 4:now pour half the chocolate shake into the serving glass
    •  
            
  • step 5:now add crushed cashews and a scoop of chocolate icecream as a layer 
  and pour the balance milk shake
    • 
            
  • step 6:top it with a scoop of ice cream along with chocolate syrup and choco chip
    • 
            
  • step 7:now add a cherry on top and serve it cold
