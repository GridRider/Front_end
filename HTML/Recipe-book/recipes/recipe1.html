NAADAN BEEF ROAST

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Beef
  • 1&1/2 tablespoon Chilli powder
  • 1&1/2 tablespoon Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon Garam Masala
  • 1&1/2 tablespoonGinger-Garlic Paste
  • 1&1/2 tablespoonPepper powder
  • salt
  • 2 Onion
  • 1 tomato
  • A small piece of ginger
  • oil

Steps:


            
  • Step 1: clean the meet properly and put it in a cooker
    • 
            
  • step 2 : into that cooker add chilli powder, Coriander powder,Turmeric powder
            Garam Masala,Ginger-Garlic Paste,salt,pepper powder,salt and give it a nice mix.
    • 
            
  • step 3 : add a little bit of water to avoid any burnings and close the cooker and 
            put it the gas stove and wait for 16 pressure cooker vissle
    • 
               
  • step 4 : In another pan add 3 table spoon of oil 
    •  
               
  • step 5 : add ginger and when the raw smell of ginger fades then add nicely chopped
            onions and tomato
    • 
               
  • step 6 : when it gets sorted add the beef with the water in the cooker which it cooked
    • 
               
  • step 7 :wait for it to get dry overtime if you want a little gravey then you should add 
          a boiling hot water into the mix after the water we added from the cooker completly 
          dried with beef
