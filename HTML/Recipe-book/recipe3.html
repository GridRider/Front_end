Recipe 3 : Sandwich

Ingredients:

  1. Bread
  2. Butter
  3. Onion
  4. Tomato
  5. Mayonize
  6. Tomato Ketchup

Steps:


            
  • Step 1:Cut onion and tomato into teeny tiny pieces
    • 
            
  • step2:Add it into a bowl.
    • 
            
  • step 3:Now into that bowl add mayonize and tomato ketchup and mix it together
    • 
            
  • step 4:its consistency is no too watery also nor too hard it must be in a creamy consistency
    •  
            
  • step 5:now take two bread and butter its one side and add fillings in the other side and close it with another bread
    • 
            
  • step 6:make as many as you like if you like cheese then add cheese on top of the filling
    • 
            
  • step 7:now toast the bread on each side
    • 
            
  • step 8:your yummy sandwich is ready to serve
