Recipe 1 : Beef Roast

Ingredients:

  1. Beef : 1kg
  2. Chilli powder : 1 and half tablespoon
  3. Coriander powder : 1 and half tablespoon
  4. Turmeric powder : half tablespoon
  5. Garam Masala : 1 tablespoon
  6. Ginger-Garlic Paste : 1 and half tablespoon
  7. Pepper powder : 1 and half tablespoon
  8. salt : According to your taste
  9. Onion : 2
  10. tomato : 1
  11. ginger : A small piece
  12. oil

Steps:


            
  • Step 1: clean the meet properly and put it in a cooker
    • 
            
  • step2: into that cooker add chilli powder, Coriander powder,Turmeric powder
Garam Masala,Ginger-Garlic Paste,salt,pepper powder,salt and give it a nice mix.
    • 
            
  • step 3: add a little bit of water to avoid any burnings and close the cooker and 
put it the gas stove and wait for 16 pressure cooker vissle
    • 
               
  • step 4: In another pan add 3 table spoon of oil 
    •  
               
  • step 5: add ginger and when the raw smell of ginger fades then add nicely chopped
onions and tomato
    • 
               
  • step 6: when it gets sorted add the beef with the water in the cooker which it cooked
    • 
               
  • step 7:wait for it to get dry overtime if you want a little then you should add 
a boiling hot water into the mix after the water we added from the cooker completly dried with beef
